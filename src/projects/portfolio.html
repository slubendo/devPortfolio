About Me

👤
Alex Morrow
Engineer · Writer · Musician · San Francisco

Craft lives
at the overlap.

I'm a software engineer, writer, and musician based in San Francisco. Twelve years into building systems at scale — from early-stage startups to infrastructure used by millions.

That belief led me to write about what I build, and eventually to make music that reflected how building made me feel. The three disciplines feed each other in ways I'm still mapping — each one an act of abstraction, a process of deciding what matters.

Currently working on open-source developer tooling, a long-form essay series on creativity and systems thinking, and finishing my third album. Available for consulting engagements in backend architecture and developer experience.

Before going independent: Staff Engineer at Linear, led the platform team at Vercel, cofounded a dev-tools startup acquired in 2022.
